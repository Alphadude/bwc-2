ABOUT US

Welcome to Life

Welcome to Beyond Worship Centre I'm so glad you're here. I'm Pastor Udochukwu Nwanma. We are a community of expressive people who have received the love of Christ and never hold back in sharing the same. Here, no matter your status and background, you will find a thriving ground to to express yourself in fellowship. Our worship services are filled with great gospel music, unbiased teaching of the word , passionate intercessions, warmth, energy and love. If there is anything we can do for you while you're with us, please let us know. We hope our services will be a blessing to you! Welcome once more..
